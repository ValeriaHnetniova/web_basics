ABOUT US

Valrin is a Ukrainian fashion house founded in 1997 by designers Val and Rina. In September 1998, VR presented its first women's clothing collection, called New Era, making a revolution in the fashion world. Gradually, VR began to create not only clothing but also perfumes and accessories.
